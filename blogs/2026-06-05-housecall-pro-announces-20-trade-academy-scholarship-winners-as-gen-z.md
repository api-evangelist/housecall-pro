Housecall Pro announced 20 recipients of its 2026 Trade Academy Scholarship Program, supporting students pursuing HVAC, electrical, and plumbing careers. Applications surged 155% year-over-year, with Gen Z representing over three-quarters of applicants. Each winner receives $2,500 in scholarships, a one-year Housecall Pro membership, and a Milwaukee Tool package.
